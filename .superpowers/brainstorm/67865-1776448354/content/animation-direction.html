Animation Direction — Which vibe fits D'Pavo?

The site already has basic scroll-reveals. We're choosing the personality of the next layer of motion.

A

Energetic / Street Bold

Fast entrance snaps, cards that pop & tilt on hover, a floating spinning pizza badge, counter numbers that count up as they enter the viewport. High energy — feels like a tropical street food festival.

Good for: grabbing attention fast, mobile-first browsing, youthful audience

B

Smooth / Cinematic

Slow parallax on the hero image, text that fades and rises elegantly, sections that crossfade as you scroll. Less "pop," more "flow." Feels premium and intentional.

Good for: brand credibility, fine-dining adjacent, desktop-focused visitors

C

Mix — Bold Entrances, Calm Interactions

Punchy staggered reveals when sections enter (fast, dramatic), but hover states stay smooth and subtle. The "street food but elevated" middle ground. Best of both.

Recommended — matches the brand: authentic and lively, but not chaotic
